Overview

MarBMS is designed to connect with compatible battery management or related hardware devices through Bluetooth and nearby device functions. Guangdong Marxon Electronics Co., LTD respects your privacy and aims to limit data handling to what is reasonably necessary to provide the app's core features.

Core Function

Bluetooth device discovery, connection, status viewing, parameter display, scanning, and firmware-related operations.

Main Principle

Use the minimum information reasonably needed to provide local device management features.

Published By

Guangdong Marxon Electronics Co., LTD

Information We May Collect

  • Bluetooth-related device information needed to discover, identify, pair with, and communicate with compatible hardware.
  • QR code or barcode scan content when you choose to use the scan feature.
  • Photo library access only if you choose to import an image for scanning or related user-initiated actions.
  • Camera access only if you choose to scan a code using the device camera.
  • Approximate or nearby-device related permission data when required by the mobile operating system to support Bluetooth discovery or device connectivity.
  • Technical information such as app version, device model, operating system version, and limited diagnostic information needed to maintain app stability and compatibility.
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting Guangdong Marxon Electronics Co., LTD for support.

MarBMS is primarily intended for local device communication. We do not intentionally collect unnecessary personal information for advertising or unrelated profiling purposes.

How We Use Information

  • To discover, connect to, and communicate with compatible Bluetooth devices.
  • To display battery, device, firmware, or diagnostic information inside MarBMS.
  • To support code scanning, device identification, and user-requested operations.
  • To improve compatibility, fix bugs, maintain security, and keep the application functioning properly.
  • To respond to customer service, legal, compliance, or safety-related requests when necessary.

Sharing of Information

Guangdong Marxon Electronics Co., LTD does not sell personal information. Information may be disclosed only in limited circumstances such as:

  • when needed to provide user-requested services or app functionality;
  • when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request;
  • when reasonably necessary to protect safety, rights, property, users, or the app's security;
  • when working with service providers that support operation, maintenance, or security under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

Data Retention and Security

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer period is required by law. Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures may be used to help protect information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You may control permissions such as Bluetooth, camera, photos, or location through your device settings.
  • You may stop using MarBMS at any time.
  • If local data is stored on your device, you may remove the app or clear app-related data through your operating system where supported.
  • You may contact Guangdong Marxon Electronics Co., LTD regarding privacy questions or lawful requests concerning your information.

Children's Privacy

MarBMS is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law to provide valid consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

Guangdong Marxon Electronics Co., LTD may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at the same page location with a revised “Last Updated” date.
